ILLUSIONS

Our brain is more complicated than we think it is. Some of the observations we make are unexplainable because these are the result of brain's glitches. Such observations are termed as optical illusions. It is fascinating to see how our brain is misled when observing optical illusions. This video contains one such illusion that defies the basic principles of optics and would leave us surprised.
