Domain Brainstorm

My domain of interest is plastic sustainability as I am interested in exploring the usage of plastic in urban cities and work on reducing its usage through awareness and recycling.

Concept

It is overwhelming to see a lot of plastic surrounding us; especially the daily use plastics are ubiquitous. They are increasingly becoming cheap substitutes for everything. If we continue to use plastics without regulation, in few years it occupies the entire planet. Oceans are already taking a toll because our regular usage of plastic. I believe this problem could be tackled by changing the behavioral patterns of consumers in reducing plastic consumption. I propose a method of peer pressure and reward system to lay foundation for people’s behavioral change in using daily plastics. My project is a campaign that allows user to actively be a part of it through peer pressure from friends and family and create an impact at individual level. I’ve also incorporated reward system to further motivate people to take part in the campaign.

Description

Blue++ is a campaign that helps people make sustainable choices over daily use plastics to reduce the overall plastic footprint in the environment.

Audience

The target audience for this project is mostly students and young professionals who tend to be influenced easily through peer pressure. Also based on the assumption that they are the ones who mostly drink coffee and eat outside, it makes sense to capture this demographics. The other set of target audience would be offline shoppers who tend to carry lot of plastic bags.
