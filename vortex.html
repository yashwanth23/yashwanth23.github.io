VORTEX

Fluid dynamics is a branch of physics that studies properties of fluids and their interactions with objects.This subject is mainly important in the field of aeronautics, medicine and power generation. Water is a crucial fluid not just for our personal needs but also for some important contributions in physics. Out of many properties of water, this video shows one of the property which is called as vortex formation. Vortices are observed in a tornado, in aircarft propellers etc. This video shows the formation of vortex in a simple system like a water bottle. Emptying a water bottle with vortex takes lesser time than emptying it by just inverting the bottle.
