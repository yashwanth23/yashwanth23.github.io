Reminiscence Clock

Creative Technology | Physical Computing | Augmented Reality

ROLE

Researcher, Art director, Developer, Designer

TEAM

Sole contributor

SKILLS

Augmented reality, Physical computing, Laser cutting

TOOLS

Arduino, Unity, Vuforia

KEYWORDS

Augmented Reality, Product Design, Digital archiving, Memories, Abstract design, Physical Computing

DESCRIPTION

We go through countless experiences every day, some of them might be big moments and some of them might be little memories that stand out because of the emotional impact they had on us. Research says that reminiscing on these small positive moments help reduce anxiety and ease the symptoms of depression. Although we have several digital technologies that archive our memories and help us look back at them, they might lead into a trap of addiction. Through this project, I explored the possibility of reminiscing memories in a story telling format without leading the users into addiction. Reminiscence clock is an abstract pattern changing physical device that changes patterns periodically and the users can access their memories by scanning the pattern this device makes, thus giving limited access while also reminding of their past.
