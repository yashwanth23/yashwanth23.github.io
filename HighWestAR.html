ABOUT

High West Consumer AR is an interactive AR experience to educate consumers about High West Whiskey brand. This was done as part of my internship at Constellation Brands.

OBJECTIVE

The aim of the project is to create fun and interactive experience of whiskey tasting for consumers while also educating them about the making process and flavor profiles of various products of the High West Whiskey brand. We chose Augmented Reality to be a medium for storytelling as it is easily accessible to anyone who owns a smart phone.
