OSCILLOART

In simple terms, oscillation is a back and forth motion of any object. We can find many examples of oscillation in nature like springs, pendulum clocks, sound vibration on speakers etc. This video is an attempt to understand oscillations of a pendulum through tracing their motion. I attempted to capture the patterns made by a pendulum which oscillates in a 2D space. Higher the dimension we go, the more complicated patterns we get. It is observed that the motion yields lissajous patterns which are related to sinusoidal curves.
