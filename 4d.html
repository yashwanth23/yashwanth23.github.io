Plastic Vision

Nowadays, plastics are extensively used almost everywhere as a cheap substitutes. Little do the people know about how much harm they are causing to the environment consciously or unconsciously because of their usage of daily plastics. This video is an attempt to help realize about the various forms of daily plastics that an average human being uses on a day to day basis. Sounds associated with every day plastic usage is also incorporated to engage the audience with more than one sense (in this case visual and auditory senses).
