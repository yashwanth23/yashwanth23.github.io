About me

I am a Product designer and a Creative technologist based in New York City. My work focuses on solving problems through an unconventional design approach while also thinking through the user's perspective. Other interests include Game design, Augmented and Virtual reality.

I graduated from Parsons School of Design (Class of 2020) with MFA in Design and Technology. I have an undergraduate degree in Mechanical Engineering and work experience as Product designer. I also pursued a one year collaboratory study at Cornell Tech in Product and Startup studio.

I like to explore and get creative with photography. During weekends you can find me hiking a trail or going on long drives. Also, when I put my headphones on, it is either Rap or Alternative rock music.
