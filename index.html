Seven in Seven

Concept 1: The Dance of Math

This concept explores the ways in which mathematics can be played around to obtain different shapes and patterns. My intent for this concept is to visualize how different bounded and periodic functions look like when drawn on a canvas. Further iterations could include hearing how different bounded and periodic functions sound like by incorporting sound libraries. Each day a customized function is formed by combining different periodic functions using various mathematical operators. This function is then used to obtain a pattern. Sound functionality can be added as well to obtain a sounding pattern.

Concept 2: Custom shape Mosiac Art

The main theme around this concept is to use customized random shapes as pixels and inturn multiplied over the canvas to create a known shape or a known figure. These shapes can be as random as simple geometrical figures to arbitrary brush strokes to complex sillhoutes of objects, humans etc. The intention here is to bring out artistic appeal of randomized shapes or patterns.

Concept 3: Stills of Physics

The idea behind this concept is to explain the laws of physics through a series of photographs(2-3 photos). Every day a different physics concept is chosen and photographs will be taken accordingly. These photographs are not taken from a simulated environment but are purely captured from nature. Some of the concepts include electrostatics, gravitation, optical illusions etc.
