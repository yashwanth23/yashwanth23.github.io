DESCRIPTION

Running with our phone is annoying. Especially these days smart phones are getting bigger and bulkier, it’s hard to carry our phone on a run and still be able to listen to streamed content. Tambr, is the first smart headphone optimized for runners that allow them to leave their phone at home without sacrificing their favorite streamed content.

PROTOTYPE

We’ve created an initial functioning prototype using a Raspberry Pi that is powered by a portable power source. This controller recieves internet through 4G LTE SIM enabled stick and runs the earphones that are bone conduction and dynamic drivers enabled. For the final product, all this technology would fit into one single chip that sits comfortably inside the Tambr earphones.
