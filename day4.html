We don't usually encounter electromagnetism in our day to day lives but has been an interesting topic for researchers in the mordern world. This is due to the fact that these concepts are used mostly in power generation and transmission. This project is an attempt to capture the unsual usage of electromagnetism that is observed recently. The video shows a girl who has a magnet embedded in her ring finger and is trying to disrupt the electric fields in the laptop.
