High West Consumer AR

Creative Technology | Augmented Reality

ROLE

Art director, Developer, 2D designer, 3D interaction designer

TEAM

Zach Darrow, Mark Foster

SKILLS

3D animations, Game programming, 3D interaction, Shaders

TOOLS

Unity, Vuforia, AR Foundation, Shader graphs

KEYWORDS

Augmented Reality, Immersive storytelling, Digital Marketing

DESCRIPTION

This is an Augmented reality project developed for High West whiskey brand as part of my internship at Constellation brands. This project is developed for iOS application using Unity and AR Foundation. The idea behind this project is to create fun experience of whiskey tasting for consumers while also educating them about various products of the brand. This experience allows the user to interact with various assets in the scene while also providing knowledge of the making process and the flavor profiles of various products that are produced at High West distillery.
