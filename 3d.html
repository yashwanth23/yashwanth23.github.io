Dustbin out of Plastic waste

I made use of the plastic bottles and cups that were lying around in my house to make something which is commonly found in every house, that is a dustbin. It is very usual to find a dustbin which is made of plastic in every household. Rather than making a new dustbin with plastic it makes complete sense to design bins with old plastic, hence reducing the overall plastic production and increasing the recycling process.
