ABOUT

This is a speculative project about the future of retail businesses. This project explores the possibility of combining online and offline experiences to give the customers a holistic degree of satisfaction while shopping for products.

OBJECTIVE

There is an increased demand for online shopping as it is convenient for consumers. But the core aspects like product experience and social interactions of offline retail is still missing in an online ecosystem. Also, as E-commerce is booming, retail stores are taking a toll, especially in places like New York City, stores are vacating due to increased rents and reduced sales.

Considering this scenario we came up with a question of "How might we create an intervention that would give consumers the offline store experience in an online ecosystem while also helping the retail stores survive?"

RESEARCH

IDEA

The idea here is to complement online shopping by providing a way to make the consumer experience the product while also helping the retail stores survive. In order to do so we propose an Augmented reality/ Mixed reality shopping experience inside the empty stores. Physically we do not see anything but digitally there is a whole new shopping experience for consumers. These experiences are personalized according to consumer's online shopping patterns. We speculate that in the future this combined with the drone delivery technology, can provide a whole new level of shopping experience to the consumers while also benefiting all the stakeholder involved.
